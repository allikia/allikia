3. Theme

The theme of the website is vibrant and modern, featuring a gradient color palette of blues and purples that evokes a galaxy-like feel. The layout is clean and organized, using flexbox for a responsive structure, with rounded corners and subtle shadows giving elements a 3D effect. Typography is simple and readable with bold headings, while interactive hover effects and animations add a dynamic touch. The overall design balances visual appeal and usability, making it easy to navigate and engaging for users, while also reflecting a professional yet creative aesthetic.
